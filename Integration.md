### Integration With Other Teams

## Menu
- Manages the items created by the menu team
- Stock levels for each item will go up and down accordingly

## Transaction
- Details of transactions will be sent to the transaction team
- Stock alerts will be sent to the transaction team

## Facilities
- Manages the items created by the facilities team
- Stock levels for each item will go up and down accordingly

## Database
- The database team stores the tables of stock information
- The database will read item information from the Inventory Management module
- Item information will be read from the database
